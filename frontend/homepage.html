About Us

We’re a team of university students who know how demanding and high-pressure exam season can be — because we’re living it too. That’s what inspired us to create this platform: a simple, effective way to generate realistic exam papers for focused revision. Built by students, for students, our tool is designed to help you prepare smarter by giving you instant access to high-quality practice papers that mirror real exam formats. No more wasting time hunting for the right past paper — just generate and go.
